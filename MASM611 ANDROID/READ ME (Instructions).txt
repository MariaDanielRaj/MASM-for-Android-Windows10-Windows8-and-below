
::: INSTALLING MASM611 IN ANDROID :::


-----------------------------------------------------------------
Apps Required:
RAR, AnDOSBox, DroidEdit Free
-----------------------------------------------------------------

1. Extract masm611.rar
2. Move files to device memory (storage/emulated/0/)
3. Open AnDOSBox and Choose "Edit .conf File"
4. Select "DroidEdit Free" app from popup menu
5. Append the following text
c:
cd masm611\bin
pwb
6. Save it
7. "Exit DroidEdit Free" and Choose "Start Emulator" in AnDOSBox
